Transforming Healthcare Data into Clinical Insight.

Our mission is to eliminate fragmented paper records and provide doctors with a centralized, secure, and longitudinal view of patient health, enabling data-driven decisions at the point of care.

Current Goal

Streamlining the Patient-Doctor encounter workflow to reduce administrative burden and increase patient interaction time.

Near Future

Implementing Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for nurse/admin workflows and integrating external Pharmacy APIs for e-prescriptions.

Long-Term Target

Developing AI-driven diagnostic suggestions based on historical data and launching a dedicated Mobile App for patient self-service.

System Architecture

Our platform is built on a robust, scalable technical stack designed for reliability and security.

  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Database: MySQL (Relational)
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5 & Jinja2
  • Security: Bcrypt Hashing & parameterized queries
  • Data Integrity: Strict Foreign Key constraints
